Frequently Asked Questions

Click on "My Requests" in the navigation menu, then click "New Request". Fill out the form with your needs and submit. You can choose to submit anonymously or directly to a specific food bank.

Food banks typically review and respond to requests within 24-72 hours. You'll receive a notification once your request has been reviewed.

An anonymous request allows you to request assistance without revealing your identity to food banks. Your request will be visible to all food banks, and they can choose to accept it.

When you receive a donation, you'll see an "Acknowledge" button on your dashboard. Click it to confirm receipt and send a thank you message to the food bank.
