Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, email, and location to facilitate food donations and matching between food banks, donors, and recipients.

2. How We Use Your Information
  • To verify your identity and manage your account.
  • To connect recipients with food banks and donors.
  • To communicate updates regarding your requests and donations.
  • To improve FoodBank Hub’s features and user experience.
3. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with relevant food banks, donors, or recipients for request and donation coordination. We do not sell or trade your personal information.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and users are encouraged to safeguard their login information.

5. Your Rights

You can request to update, download, or delete your data anytime through your account settings or by contacting support.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Major updates will be communicated via the platform or email notifications.
