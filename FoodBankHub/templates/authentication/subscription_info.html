Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?
You get full access to all platform features for 3 months completely free. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to subscribe to continue using the platform.
How do I pay for my subscription?
You can pay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash. Simply upload your payment evidence (screenshot or receipt), and our admin team will verify and activate your subscription within 24 hours.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens if my subscription expires?
You can still log in to your account, but you won't be able to create requests, manage donations, or access other platform features until you renew your subscription.
Is there a discount for yearly plans?
Yes! The yearly plan costs KSH 10,000 (equivalent to less than KSH 834/month), saving you KSH 14,000 compared to paying monthly.
How long does payment verification take?
Our admin team typically verifies payments within 24 hours. You'll receive a notification once your payment is approved and your subscription is activated.
