Privacy Policy - Food Banks
1. Information We Collect

We collect organizational and contact information such as food bank name, address, phone number, email, and uploaded documents to verify your eligibility and facilitate recipient requests.

2. How We Use Your Information
  • To verify your food bank and authorize access to the platform.
  • To connect your food bank with recipients and donors.
  • To communicate important updates regarding requests or platform policies.
3. Data Sharing

Your information will be shared only with relevant recipients, donors, or FoodBank Hub staff to facilitate requests. Data will not be sold or shared with unauthorized third parties.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your data, including encrypted storage and restricted access. However, FoodBank Hub cannot guarantee complete protection against unauthorized access.

You may request to update, correct, or delete your account information at any time by contacting our support team.

FoodBank Hub may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of significant changes via email or the platform.
