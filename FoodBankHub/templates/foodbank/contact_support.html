Frequently Asked Questions

Go to "Requests" in your navigation menu to view all incoming requests. You can filter by status, urgency, or date. Click on any request to view details and choose to accept or decline it.

Use the "Donations" section to track incoming donations. You can mark donations as received, processed, and distributed. Update the status to keep donors and recipients informed of the progress.

The "Analytics" section provides comprehensive insights into your food bank's operations. You can view donation trends, request patterns, and impact metrics. Use the "Reports" section to generate detailed reports for stakeholders.

You can send messages to recipients through the request management system. When you accept a request, you can include pickup instructions, requirements, or any other relevant information.

Click on your food bank name in the navigation menu and select "Settings". You can update your contact information, operating hours, service areas, and notification preferences.

We provide comprehensive training materials, video tutorials, and best practice guides. Contact our support team to schedule training sessions for your staff or volunteers.
