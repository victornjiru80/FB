CO₂ Emissions Calculation Methodology
Food Waste Prevention
  • Free Food: 1.0 kg per unit (100% waste prevention)
  • Subsidized Food: 0.8 kg per unit (80% waste prevention)
Formula:
Waste Prevented = (Free Food × 1.0) + (Subsidized Food × 0.8)
CO₂ Calculation
  • Food: 1 kg waste = 2.5 kg CO₂ equivalent
  • Non-Food: 1 unit = 0.5 kg CO₂ equivalent
Formula:
CO₂ Saved (tons) = (Waste Prevented × 2.5) ÷ 1000
Note: Calculations based on UNEP (United Nations Environment Programme) and WRAP (Waste and Resources Action Programme) benchmarks for food waste and carbon emissions.
